RECTOR NUST INAUGURATES THE NATIONAL CYBER SECURITY AUDITING AND EVALUATION LAB MCS

Lt Gen Naweed Zaman, HI (M), (Retd), Rector NUST, inaugurated the National Cyber Security Auditing & Evaluation Lab (NCSAEL) at the NUST Military College of Signals (MCS), Rawalpindi, on August 23, 2019. This lab is part of the National Centre for Cyber Security (NCCS), one of the 4 national centres of excellence being established at Pakistani universities under an initiative of the Govt of Pakistan.
